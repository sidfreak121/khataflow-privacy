KhataFlow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. Introduction

KhataFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance app designed for users in Pakistan. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using KhataFlow you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the app.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name and email address (when you create an account)
  • Financial records you enter — account balances, transactions, khata entries, split bills
  • Passwords (stored securely by Firebase; we never see them)

Information collected automatically:

  • App crash reports and error logs (device type, OS version, error trace)
  • We do NOT collect location, contacts, photos, or microphone data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync the KhataFlow service across your devices
  • To restore your data when you reinstall or switch devices
  • To improve app stability through anonymised crash reports

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), operated by Google LLC. Google may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Financial records you enter are stored:

  • Locally on your device (AsyncStorage) for offline access
  • In Firebase Firestore (cloud) when you are signed in

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Firestore security rules ensure each user can only access their own data
  • Passwords are hashed and managed by Firebase Authentication — we never store raw passwords

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Access or export your data from within the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us (see Section 9)
  • Use the app in offline mode without creating an account — your data then stays only on your device

After account deletion, your data is removed from Firebase within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

KhataFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
